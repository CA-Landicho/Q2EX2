Impulse

  • Product of net force acting on an object and time interval during which force is applied
  • Vector quantity
  • Direction is the same as the net force
  • SI unit is N/s, kg m/s, or J (joules)
  • formula: J=Ft
  • F is Force, t is time

Momentum

  • Product of mass and velocity
  • Describes an object's quantity of motion
  • Direction is the same as velocity (v)
  • SI unit is kg m/s
  • Formula: p=mv
  • m is mass, v is velocity

Valence Electrons

  • Electrons found in the outermost shell of the atom
  • Found in the valence shell
  • Number of valence electrons of elements increases from left to right in the periodic table
  • When counting valence electrons, Group A (Groups 1, 2 and 13-18) is only considered
